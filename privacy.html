Privacy Policy

Effective date: 17 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the 4 Ever app (“the app,” “we,” “us”) handles information when you use it. By using the app, you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

4 Ever does not require an account and does not ask you for personal information such as your name or email to play.

Your game progress and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not collect or upload them.

The app uses third-party services that may collect limited information automatically, as described below.

Advertising

The app shows advertising through Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, AdMob may collect information such as a device advertising identifier, IP address, general device information, and app usage data. We do not control what AdMob collects, and we do not receive this information in identifiable form.

You can learn more in Google’s privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy and Google’s advertising information at policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

Purchases

Purchases (such as the one-time “No Ads” upgrade) are processed by Apple through the App Store. Apple handles your payment details — we never see or store your payment information. The app only records whether the upgrade is active, stored locally on your device.

Your choices

You can limit ad tracking at any time in your device settings (on iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking). You can also remove ads entirely with the in-app “No Ads” purchase.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through a third-party service in the app, please contact us so it can be addressed.

Data retention & security

Because game data stays on your device, you control it — deleting the app removes it. Information handled by Google and Apple is governed by their respective policies and security practices.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
