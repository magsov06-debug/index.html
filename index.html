Frequently asked

How do I play?

Combine all four numbers with +, −, ×, ÷ and parentheses to reach the target. The key trick: leave the gap between two numbers empty and they join into one — 4 and 4 become 44.

How do I remove ads?

Tap the “NO ADS?” button at the top of the main menu to make the one-time No Ads purchase. It also makes skipping levels free.

I bought No Ads but it’s gone (or I got a new phone)

Open Settings inside the app and tap “Restore Purchases” to bring it back at no extra cost.

How do I reset my progress?

Open Settings and tap “Reset Progress.” Note this can’t be undone.
